## ğŸ¨ Ethical Advertising (If Needed)

Only consider ads if subscription revenue proves insufficient. If you must:

### **Sponsored Event Templates**

**How It Works:**
- Event management companies sponsor pre-made templates
- Users can optionally use branded templates
- Appears as "Sponsored Templates" section
- Never forced, always clearly labeled

**Example:**
- Eventbrite sponsors "Conference Template"
- Zoom sponsors "Virtual Meeting Template"
- Canva sponsors "Workshop Template"

**Revenue:** $500-2,000/month per sponsor

**User Experience:**
- Clearly labeled as "Sponsored by [Brand]"
- Enhances value (better templates)
- Optional and non-intrusive
- Maintains black & white aesthetic

---

### **Partner Event Suggestions (Very Light)**

**How It Works:**
- After creating an event, show 1 relevant suggestion
- "Planning a conference? Check out these Eventbrite events in your area"
- Affiliate link to partner

**Requirements:**
- Maximum 1 suggestion per session
- Must be genuinely relevant
- Easy to dismiss
- Never interrupts core workflow

**Revenue:** $0.10-0.50 per click (affiliate commissions)
